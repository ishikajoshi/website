About Me
Hey There! I am Ishika and I am a Researcher in Human Computer Interaction . I am currently working as HCI Research Associate at Adobe! Priorly, I have been associated with some very cool research at @IIITD, Cornell Tech, Melange Lab, Weave Lab. My research pans over topics like Social Computing, Interactive Computing and HCAI. Before joining Adobe, I graduated from IIITD with a Bachelor in Technology in Computer Science and Design where I studied both design and cs closely along with the intersections of the two.
During my Undergrad, I worked heavily with Dr. Pushpendra Singh on Social Computing Research at the Melange Lab. I also did research Interactive Technologies (Interaction Design, AR/VR, Gamification for Education and Storytelling) with Dr. Aman Parnami at the Weave Lab. I collaborated with Dr. Dhruv Kumar for research on Human AI Interaction problems as well.
I also collaborated with Dr. Tapan Parikh of Cornell Tech on multiple projects panning over ICTD and Human AI Interaction problems.
At Adobe, my current research is aimed at building for Human AI interaction by studying Human ‚Äì LLM Interactions. I have also worked on interactions for annotation practices for GenAI applications and culturalisation of Adobe Express.
